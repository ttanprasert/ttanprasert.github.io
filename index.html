About me

Thitaree "Mint" Tanprasert is a PhD student at Department of Computer Science, University of British Columbia (UBC) and a member of Designing for People (DFP) research cluster. She completed her bachelor’s degree in mathematics and computer science from Harvey Mudd College in 2019. Her broad research area is Human Computer Interaction (HCI), specifically the intersection of machine learning, multimedia, and education.
